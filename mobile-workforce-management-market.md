<p><p>Mobile Workforce Management (MWM) solutions are primarily categorized into two types: cloud-based and on-premise. Cloud-based MWM systems offer scalability and flexibility, enabling users to access data and functionalities from any device with internet connectivity, often with lower upfront costs.
