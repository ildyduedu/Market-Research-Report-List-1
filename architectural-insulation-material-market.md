<p><p>The architectural insulation material market comprises several types, each offering unique benefits. Rock wool, made from natural stone, provides excellent fire resistance and soundproofing. Glass wool, created from recycled glass, is lightweight and effective for thermal insulation. EPS (expanded polystyrene) and XPS (extruded polystyrene) are rigid foam boards known for their moisture resistance and thermal efficiency. PU (polyurethane) and PIR (polyisocyanurate) offer high insulation values with lower thickness. Other materials may include cellulose and mineral fiber, catering to specific insulation needs.</p></p>
