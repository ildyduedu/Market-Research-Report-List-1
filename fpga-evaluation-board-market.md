<p><ul><li>SRAM</li><li>FLASH</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The FPGA evaluation board market can be categorized into SRAM and Flash types, each with distinct characteristics. SRAM-based FPGAs offer higher performance and faster processing capabilities, making them suitable for demanding applications like signal processing and telecommunications. In contrast, Flash-based FPGAs provide non-volatile storage, allowing for configuration retention without power, making them ideal for low-power applications and remote deployments. Both types cater to diverse industries, including automotive, consumer electronics, and industrial automation, supporting various design and development needs.</p></p>
