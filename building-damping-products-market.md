<p><p>The building damping products market includes various types designed to reduce structural vibrations. Viscous and visco-elastic dampers utilize materials that dissipate energy through fluid movement or deformation. Tuned mass dampers involve large masses that counteract vibrations at specific frequencies, while tuned liquid dampers use moving fluids to achieve similar effects. Liquid dampers rely solely on fluid movement for vibration control. Other market products may incorporate advanced technologies or hybrid approaches to enhance structural stability and performance.</p></p>
