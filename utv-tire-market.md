<p><p>The UTV tire market is characterized by three primary types: pneumatic, solid, and polyurethane. Pneumatic tires are air-filled, offering better traction and shock absorption, making them ideal for rough terrains. Solid tires, on the other hand, are puncture-proof and durable, suitable for heavy-duty applications but may compromise ride comfort. Polyurethane tires blend flexibility with durability, providing a lightweight option that resists wear and tear, making them versatile for various terrains, especially in industrial settings.</p></p>
