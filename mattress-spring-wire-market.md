<p><p>The mattress spring wire market consists of several types of coil systems that provide varying levels of support and comfort. Continuous coils feature a single wire forming interconnected coils for stability. Bonnell coils, structured in an hourglass shape, offer balanced support but may not conform well to body shapes. Offset coils use an hourglass design with flattened tops for better contouring. Marshall coils, individually wrapped, provide targeted support and motion isolation, enhancing comfort and durability in mattresses.</p></p>
