<p><ul><li>Shunt Capacitive Compensation</li><li>Shunt Inductive Compensation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Flexible Shunt Compensation Market consists of two primary types: Shunt Capacitive Compensation and Shunt Inductive Compensation. Shunt Capacitive Compensation enhances voltage stability and reduces reactive power demand by injecting reactive power into the system, making it beneficial for improving power quality. In contrast, Shunt Inductive Compensation absorbs excess reactive power, helping to stabilize voltage levels and improve system reliability. Both types play crucial roles in optimizing electrical networks and ensuring efficient energy distribution in power systems.</p></p>
