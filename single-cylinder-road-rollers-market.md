<p><ul><li>Tamp Drum</li><li>Smooth Drum</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The single-cylinder road rollers market features primarily two types: tamp drum and smooth drum. Tamp drum rollers are equipped with protruding, heavy tamping feet that compact soil or asphalt surfaces effectively, making them ideal for sub-grade and base layer work. In contrast, smooth drum rollers have a uniform, flat surface designed for finishing tasks, focusing on achieving a smooth, dense surface layer in asphalt and soil applications. Both types play crucial roles in construction and infrastructure development.</p></p>
