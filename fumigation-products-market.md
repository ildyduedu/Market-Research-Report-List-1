<p><p>The fumigation products market can be categorized into three types: solid, liquid, and gas. Solid fumigants, such as pellets or tablets, are typically used for structural fumigation and can release gas upon heating. Liquid fumigants are often applied by spraying or soaking, making them suitable for specific applications like soil treatment. Gas fumigants, which include various volatile compounds, penetrate materials effectively and are used for large-scale pest control, especially in shipping containers and warehouses. Each type serves distinct purposes based on environmental and application needs.</p></p>
