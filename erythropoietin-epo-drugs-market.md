<p><p>The Erythropoietin (EPO) drugs market primarily comprises two types: Epoetin-alfa and Darbepoetin-alfa. Both are critical in managing anemia related to renal impairments or cancer treatments, driving significant demand in the pharmaceutical market.</p></p>
