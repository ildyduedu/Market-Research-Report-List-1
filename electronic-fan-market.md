<p><ul><li>Axial Electronic Fan</li><li>Centrifugal Electronic Fan</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electronic fan market is primarily divided into three types: Axial electronic fans, which move air parallel to the fan's axis, providing high airflow at low pressure, making them ideal for cooling applications. Centrifugal electronic fans, on the other hand, draw air in at the center and expel it at an angle, generating higher pressure and suitable for applications requiring airflow through ducts. Other markets include specialty fans, such as mixed-flow and blower-type fans, serving varied industrial and commercial needs.</p></p>
