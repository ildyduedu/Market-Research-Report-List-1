<p><p>The Micro-Lens for UV-LED market includes two primary types: hemisphere lenses and flat lenses. Hemisphere lenses are designed to focus and distribute UV light effectively, enhancing illumination uniformity across surfaces. They are often utilized in applications requiring precision, such as printing and curing. Flat lenses, on the other hand, provide a more direct light beam with less distortion, making them ideal for specific tasks that demand concentrated UV light. Both types cater to diverse industrial needs, driving innovation in UV-LED technologies.</p></p>
