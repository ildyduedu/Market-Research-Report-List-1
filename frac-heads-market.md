<p><p>Frac heads are essential components in hydraulic fracturing, designed to manage fluid flow during well stimulation. The flanged style frac head features a robust flange connection, promoting secure attachment and ease of installation, suitable for high-pressure operations. Threaded style frac heads utilize threaded connections, allowing for flexibility in assembly and disassembly, typically in lower-pressure applications. Inlet style frac heads focus on optimizing fluid entry points, enabling efficient distribution and flow management, making them vital for maximizing fracture effectiveness.</p></p>
