<p><p>The Connected Vehicle Gateway (CVG) market comprises two primary segments: Central Gateways and LAN Gateways. Central Gateways manage communication between vehicle systems and external networks, ensuring data flows efficiently across multiple services while enhancing cybersecurity. LAN Gateways, on the other hand, focus on local area networks within the vehicle, facilitating connection between embedded systems and devices, such as sensors and infotainment units. Both types play a crucial role in enabling vehicle connectivity and supporting advanced automotive applications.</p></p>
