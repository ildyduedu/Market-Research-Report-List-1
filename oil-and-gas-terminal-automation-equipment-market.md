Programmable Logic Controllers (PLC) and Distributed Control Systems (DCS) manage equipment automation and control. Human-Machine Interfaces (HMI) provide user interaction and monitoring. Blending Controllers optimize product mixing, and Safety & Security systems ensure protection against hazards and unauthorized access, contributing to robust terminal operations.</p></p>
