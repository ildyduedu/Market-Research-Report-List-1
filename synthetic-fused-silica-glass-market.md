<p><p>The synthetic fused silica glass market encompasses various manufacturing processes, primarily the Chemical Vapor Deposition (CVD) and Plasma Deposition processes. The CVD process involves depositing a thin film of silica from gaseous precursors, resulting in high purity and uniformity, making it ideal for optics and semiconductor applications. In contrast, the Plasma Deposition process utilizes plasma to create silica films, offering enhanced control over material properties. Both methods are crucial for producing advanced silica glass used in high-tech industries.</p></p>
